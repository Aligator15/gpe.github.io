Frequently Ask Questions

Why joining us?

- Active in TMP as well as in Convoys.
- A kind and experienced family.
- We place great emphasis on support and helpfulness.
- Offering a custom interior and UI.
- Doing our best in what we do, which is driving together and having fun!
- Offering Tracker & Drivers Hub!
- And of course, maintain our trucks and drive jobs in honour of the Golden Phoenix.

Our Requirements

- You must always adhere to the TruckersMP and Discord Rules.
- You should have the ability to understand and communicate in English.
- You must have a minimum of 250 Hours in Euro Truck Simulator2.
- You must be at least 16 years old. (Exceptions can be made based on maturity.)
- Your TruckersMP Account must be at least 3 months old.
- We offer 2 options for our drivers to meet the monthly requirements. Attend 3 Convoys at your choice.Attend 1 Convoy + 2000 Km logged distance each month.We may offer other options upon contact with our management team.
- You must be able to attend and drive in at least 3 Convoys GPE is attending each month.
- You must always be respectful to one another and behave in a positive manner.
- Must be willing to use GPE's Paint Job at all public events.
- Must be willing to use GPE's Tag all the time unless granted otherwise such as helping at an external event.
- Must be willing to join the VTLog VTC.
- You should respect all other players in TMP by driving professionally and respectfully.
- You should always uphold the reputation of GPE within the community, which means, respect all TruckersMP Rules and behaving well in all areas.
